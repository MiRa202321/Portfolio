About Me

A learner at heart; An engineer in action

I’m Rashmi, a Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence undergraduate at KIET Group of Institutions, graduating in 2026 with a strong academic foundation (GPA: 8.7). Over the past few years, I’ve cultivated my skills across multiple domains — from Java, Kotlin, SQL and Python. My journey has been hands-on: I’ve developed Android applications, data-driven ML models and end-to-end projects that merge technology with real-world problem-solving.

I thrive on building solutions that are both practical and impactful. Some of my key projects include AyurConnect, a platform connecting Ayurvedic doctors and patients with integrated health prediction models; a Gold Price Prediction system using Random Forest regression; and several Android applications such as a Weather App and Calculator App built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. Each of these projects deepened my understanding of how technologies like APIs, databases and scalable architectures come together to create meaningful applications.

Beyond technical work, I actively contribute to my campus community as a Core Lead at Innogeeks Club, where I organize events, bootcamps and collaborative projects. These experiences strengthened my leadership, teamwork and communication skills. I’m also continuously expanding my toolkit, having explored Spring Boot, Tableau and AWS. I’m eager to grow as a native android developer and to apply my skills at the exciting intersection of technology and data-driven insights.
